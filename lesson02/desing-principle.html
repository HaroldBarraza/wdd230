Visual Hierarchy

Apple Inc

apple.com

Apple's website exemplifies visual hierarchy through the use of large, bold typography, striking imagery, and strategic placement of elements. Important information such as product names and calls to action are emphasized through size, color, and positioning, guiding the user's attention effectively.

White Space and Clean Design

Dropbox

dropbox.com

Dropbox's website showcases a clean and minimalistic design with effective use of white space. The layout is uncluttered, and content is given room to breathe. The generous spacing between elements contributes to a visually pleasing and user-friendly interface.

PARC: Alignment

Amazon

amazon.com

Amazon's website is a good example of effective alignment. Key elements such as the logo, search bar, and categories are consistently aligned at the top of the page. Additionally, products within lists and grids are presented in an aligned manner, creating an organized and easy-to-explore design. Consistent alignment across the page contributes to visual clarity and enhances the user experience when interacting with the online shopping platform.
