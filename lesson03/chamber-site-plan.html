The Chamber of Commerce of Peru

Site Pourpose

The Chamber of Commerce of Peru is a national organization with political, economic and administrative autonomy, constituted in accordance with Peruvian regulations and created in order to promote, strengthen and implement actions for the development of the country's business sector.

This website aim to:

  1. Facilitate business-to-business networking.
  2. Provide information on the chamber's board of directors.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?
